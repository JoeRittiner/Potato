Ears¶

Ears handle incoming messages.

Heartbeat Ear¶

A simple Ear that sends heartbeat messages at regular intervals.

Discord Chat Ear¶

An Ear that listens to Discord messages and sends them to the Brain. Listening can be enabled and disabled via the /listen and /stop commands.
